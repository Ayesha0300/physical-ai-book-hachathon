# Citation Management System

## APA Format Examples

### Journal Article
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical*, volume(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xx.xxx/yyyy

### Book
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work: Capital letter also for subtitle*. Publisher Name.

### Web Page
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Date). Title of webpage. Site Name. URL

### Chapter in Edited Book
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter. In A. Editor & B. Editor (Eds.), *Title of book* (pp. xx-xx). Publisher Name.

## Citation Standards for This Book

- All citations must follow APA 7th edition format
- Include DOI when available
- Verify all URLs are accessible
- Include access dates for web resources
- Maintain consistent citation style throughout
